Bernstein-Rein Interactivism

Production Designer, Animator

Production design and time-line based animation for the Bernstein-Rein Interactivism campaign. The campaign provides insight to the Bernstein-Rein interactive process and was produced as a website and Flash projector for a customized USB Flash drive. The campaign received a Gold Addy at the national, regional and local Addy Awards.
